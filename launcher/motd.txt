Welcome to Crusader Kings II version 2.3! This update introduces a new way to access diplomacy in the interface, in addition to a number of other fixes and improvements affecting performance, gameplay and the history database.

Be sure to check out the new DLC - Crusader Kings II: Way of Life - which opens up new possibilities for character customization and strengthening of the roleplaying dimension of the game. You can now own a dog, seduce your liege, break your spouse out of prison, fight duels and many other things.
